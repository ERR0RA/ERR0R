Algebra

Lets start of with simple equations . An example of a simple equasion is x + 3 = 8

To work this out we need to bring 3 to the oither side , so we subtract three from both sides to get x + 3 -3 + 8 - 3

This makes x = 5

Here is another example (the answer will be told later in the harder questions) x + 3 = 8

Now let's look at another equation , but with a negative sign .

x - 5 = 9

To remove the five we need to add five to the 'x' side , and add five to the oter side.

So we get x - 5 + 5 = 9 + 5

This gets us to x = 9 +5

So x = 14
